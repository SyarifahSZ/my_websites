Education History

My academic journey from high school until my future degree plans.

High School Education

All about school

My high school education journey was one of the most memorable and meaningful experiences in my life. I studied at Sekolah Menengah Agama Rembau, which is now known as Sekolah Menengah Agama Haji Ibrahim Rembau. I spent five and a half years learning, growing, and building strong friendships.

High school was a time when I not only focused on my studies but also discovered my interests and potential. As a school prefect, I learned many valuable lessons such as time management, discipline, and respect for others.

During my time in high school, I studied subjects such as Lughah Al-Muasirah, Usul Al-Din, As-Syariah, Economy, Mathematics, Science, History, and Bahasa Melayu. My favorite subject was Economics because I found it both interesting and challenging.

Diploma Education

My education journey at the diploma level has been an important and rewarding chapter in my life. I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara, Kampus Rembau, Cawangan Negeri Sembilan.

During my diploma studies, I learned various subjects such as Records Management, Information Technology, Communication Skills, and Management. These subjects helped me understand both theoretical and practical aspects of my course.

One of the most memorable experiences was my industrial training (internship), where I gained real working experience, improved my communication skills, and learned professional responsibility.

Future Degree Plan

After completing my diploma, my next goal is to pursue a Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) Information Systems Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara, Kampus Puncak Perdana.

Continuing my education is important to me because learning is a lifelong journey. Through this degree, I hope to gain deeper knowledge and prepare myself for a professional career in the future.
