Biodata

My name is Syarifah Zahidah binti Suarel, and I was born on 29th March 2005 at Hospital Seremban, Negeri Sembilan. I am currently a student at Universiti Teknologi Mara, Kampus Rembau, Cawangan Negeri Sembilan, where I am pursuing my studies in a Diploma in Information Management. I come from a loving family of five people, and I am very grateful for their support and encouragement in everything I do.

I consider myself an introvert with someone who is a stranger to me, but an extrovert who is close to me, so it is an ambivert. I enjoy learning new things and always try to give my best in every situation. My hobbies include playing badminton, watching movies, and watching YouTube. These activities help me relax and express my creativity.

Overall, I am not particularly proud of who I am today, but I am grateful for what I have, as some people cannot achieve what I have. I look forward to improving myself and building a bright future.

High School

My education journey in high school was one of the most memorable and meaningful experiences in my life. I studied at Sekolah Menengah Agama Rembau, but now it has changed to Sekolah Menengah Agama Haji Ibrahim Rembau, where I spent five and a half of learning, growing, and building strong friendships. High school was a time when I not only focused on my studies but also discovered my interests and potential. I am the one as a school prefect, and it taught me many valuable lessons. I learned the importance of time management, discipline, and respect for others.

During my time in high school, I studied various subjects such as Lughah Al-Muasirah, Usul Al-Din, As-Syariah, Economy as an additional subject, Mathematics, Science, History, and Bahasa Melayu. Among all these subjects, my favorite is Economics because I find it both interesting and challenging. My teachers were very supportive and always encouraged me to do my best in every subject.

Diploma

My education journey at the diploma level has been an important and rewarding chapter in my life. I am currently pursuing a Diploma in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara, Kampus Rembau, Cawangan Negeri Sembilan. This program has provided me with the opportunity to gain both academic knowledge and practical experience that are valuable for my future career.

During my diploma studies, I learned various subjects related to my field, such as Records Management, Information Technology, Communication Skills, and Management. These subjects helped me understand the theoretical and practical aspects of my course. I also developed important skills such as critical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving through assignments, group projects, and presentations.

One of the most memorable parts of my diploma education was my industrial training (internship). It allowed me to experience real working conditions and apply the knowledge I had learned in class. I gained confidence, improved my communication skills, and learned how to manage responsibilities in a professional environment.

Future Degree
