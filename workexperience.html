Work Experience

A summary of my teaching and internship experiences

Teaching Experience

My work experience as a teacher in a kindergarten and as a KAFA teacher has been one of the most meaningful experiences in my life. Teaching young children requires patience, creativity, and dedication, and I am grateful for the opportunity to guide and educate them during their early learning years.

At the kindergarten, I was responsible for teaching basic subjects such as reading, writing, counting, and moral values. I also planned fun learning activities to make lessons more interesting and enjoyable for the children. Through this experience, I learned how to communicate effectively with young learners and create a positive learning environment.

Besides teaching in kindergarten, I also taught KAFA (Kelas Agama Fardhu Ain) classes. In these classes, I focused on teaching Islamic knowledge, prayers, and good manners. This experience strengthened my understanding of Islamic education and improved my ability to teach religious values with kindness and patience.

Internship Experience

My internship experience was one of the most valuable parts of my educational journey. It gave me the opportunity to apply what I had learned in class to a real working environment and helped me gain a better understanding of my future career.

I completed my internship at Pejabat Pendidikan Daerah Rembau, where I worked for two months as an intern in the Sektor Perancangan. The internship taught me the importance of discipline, responsibility, and maintaining a positive attitude at work.

I had the chance to observe how an organization operates and how teamwork contributes to achieving goals. My supervisors and colleagues were very supportive and guided me whenever I faced challenges.

Through this experience, I gained more confidence and a clearer vision of my career path. It made me realize that every task, no matter how small, plays a role in the success of an organization. I also learned to appreciate the value of continuous learning and self-improvement.
